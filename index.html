I’m Elea

Welcome to my page. I’m a curious student: music is at the heart of my days — I love discovering it, sharing it, and being in those moments when a melody or a live show brings together the people who matter to me.

Concerts & live

Nothing beats the sound filling the room, the lights, and the crowd singing in unison. Every night out at a show makes me want to explore new venues and genres, and to keep memories you can’t quite get at home.

Listening & discovery

I can spend hours digging through albums, playlists, and up-and-coming artists. I love when a track catches me off guard, when a voice pulls me in, or when a song becomes the soundtrack to an entire week.

Practice & creation

Playing, singing, or just messing around with melodic ideas is how I express myself. Music pushes me to be patient, to repeat, and to savor it when everything clicks — even for a few seconds.
